Ticket rules

  1. Enter a clear and descriptive title for your ticket.
  2. Provide a detailed description of the issue or request.
  3. Choose the appropriate category for your ticket:
    • Hardware: Issues related to physical devices.
    • Software: Problems with software applications or systems.
    • Network: Connectivity or network-related issues.
  4. If necessary, attach relevant images or files to help explain the problem.
  5. Assign the ticket priority level based on the urgency and impact of the issue.
  6. Provide any additional information or context that may be helpful for resolving the ticket.
  7. Keep the ticket status updated and respond promptly to any follow-up questions or updates.
  8. Communicate with relevant stakeholders or teams as needed for resolution.
